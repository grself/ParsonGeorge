Parson George's

Tombstone After Dark Ghost Tour

Welcome to Parson George's Tombstone After Dark Ghost Tour website! The tour is a one-hour narrated trolley ride that takes guests to the most haunted spots in Tombstone, Arizona. Along the way, I tell stories of killing, lynching, murder, and paranormal activity. This website is a supplement for that tour and includes stories, photographs, links to Google maps, along with my personal theories about where ghosts come from and how they operate so guests who are interested in the paranormal can explore Tombstone's haunted locations from home. Welcome to Tombstone!

Visitors who are returning to this website may want to check out About The Site to see what I've changed since your last visit.

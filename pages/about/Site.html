This Site

Background

Over the years, I've gathered up hundreds of photographs, scores of stories, and a handful of theories about paranormal activity in Tombstone and I wanted a place where I could share those things with guests on my tour (and others who may be interested). So I grabbed a domain name and created my website.

This site began as a Facebook page, but I soon felt that Facebook was too restrictive and it was unusable by guests during the trolley tour. So I decided to create an Android app and I made that available for a few months (I don't think many people actually downloaded it). Finally, I decided to create a website based on that app for my guests to enjoy. It is designed to be legible on large screens, like a home computer, and small screens, like a tablet or phone.

I use CoffeeCup's HTML Editor to code the site and GIMP to help prepare the photos that are displayed. I use Bootstrap services to make the site responsive to the device screen size. Finally, I use a simple PHP script to process submissions from my Contact Me page.

I do not advertise on this site nor do I have any other way to make money with it (like selling visitor's email addresses). This is, truly, a labor of love and my only goal is that you enjoy a few minutes exploring Tombstone's haunted locations. If you have photos or stories about your visit to Tombstone please consider sending them along to me. I always enjoy getting that sort of feedback from guests on my trolley tour.

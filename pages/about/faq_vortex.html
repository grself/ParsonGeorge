Vortex at 5th and Allen Streets

Vortex at 5th and Allen Streets. A vortex looks like a streak of light in a photograph and this photo has a clear vortex near its center. This photo is interesting because it also has a plasma in the top right corner. As long as the orbs continue to gather energy a ghost will move from Stage Two (plasma) to Stage Three (vortex). A vortex tends to start as a blurred nebulous glow then become more defined and human-shaped as the orbs pump in more energy. In this photo, the vortex at the top-right would be young and still undefined while the plasma near the center is more mature. The dividing line between stages two and three is not absolute and it is sometimes just a "best guess" to know when a ghost has crossed from one stage to the next. Thus, this photo may well show two vortexes, one young and blurred and the other older and sharp; but I tend to believe that it is a vortex and plasma in the same photo.

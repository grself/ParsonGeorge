Stories

I've written many original ghost stories to tell while we are stopped at Boothill during the tour. I like to call these "campfire" stories since they are the type of stories that I would have heard as a boy while camping with family or friends. I've posted some of my favorite of these stories on this page for you to enjoy. For each story, you will find both an audio version of me reading the story and a printable version for those who want to read it themselves. All of these stories, like the site, is licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution License so please just give me credit for the story if you want to use it at your own campfire (or graveyard) event.

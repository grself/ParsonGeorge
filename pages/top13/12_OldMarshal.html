Old Marshal's Office

Fifth Street, Near Toughnut

This is said to be the 12th most haunted spot in Tombstone.

This landmark is located on Fifth street next to Margarita's Resturant near the corner of Toughnut Street. We know that at one time the Marshal's office was located somewhere in this area and one of the doors next to Margarita's is marked "Marshal," but we do not know if that is exactly where his office was located. However, we do know that at least one prisoner decided to hang himself in the Marshal's jail rather than face a judge in our Court House and maybe that spirit is returning today. Guests frequently get a photograph of a shadowman or other apparition standing against the wall near the door marked "Marshal's Office."

Tour Information: Our trolley passes the Old Marshal's Office and I point the location out to our guests. However, we do not stop here. It is easy to access and is less than a block from Allen Street so I encourage my guests to walk over and take pictures after the tour.
